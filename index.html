Calculation of phonetic similaritá

Vu necessa pastan phonetic transcription of Interalen vord in International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA) in linea "Vord-hypothese". Com vord posse esser reducet intro phonetic scription, Vu posse cognosceskan in libre "International auxiliari lingua INTERAL", sectionus A) Alphabet (§3) e 10. Regules of lection (§42–58).

In linea "Vord in alter lingue" pasta se phonetic transcription in IPA in alquel ethnic lingue. Infra es indicat saytes por divers lingues, che quel Vu posse trovar phonetic transcription of desirat vord.

  • German, hispan, fransese, russ, grec — wiktionary_pron
  • Italian — easypronunciation.com
